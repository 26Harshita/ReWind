Re visualise your Memories


Going out,chilling and having fun outside has become a dream these days,thanks to COVID. All we are left with is the memories of those good old days when life was normal,memories of going to the beach and the waves hitting your feet or memories of successfully making it to the final point of the long tiring trek. Who wouldn’t want to relive these again? Go back in time with ReWind and visualise your memories again with our AR ,hoping it makes the lockdown a little less gloomy.





Education with ReWind


Education has always been about sitting in classrooms, and listening to the teacher teaching using black/white boards-a 2D way of teaching. With COVID-19 spreading all over the world,even this way of teaching sounds like something of the past.Now it is all about sitting in front of our laptops wishing if we could understand the concepts better. ReWind has come up with a way to interest you by offering a platform where teachers can upload the video content and with our Augmented Reality feature students can visualize and learn it in 3-dimension.





Virtually Place your Art Pieces


Designing is no joke and an improper design might ruin the look of the entire room. We all love experimenting with the decor of our rooms but it's always uncertain how it will turn out to be. With ReWind, we give you an option to see how a picture/painting/decor you desire to add to your room will look in 3-dimension with our Augmented Reality, just so that you don’t make the wrong purchase. Happy designing with ReWind!
